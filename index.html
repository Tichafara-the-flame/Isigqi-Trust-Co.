Our Main Service

ESTATE ADMINISTRATION

Estate Planning: Estate Planning is the management of your assets and their disposal while one is still alive for the events of your death or incapacitation. When this occurs, your estate will be well kept and administered per your wishes. Wills and Trusts as tools of Estate Planning Wills: A Will is a legal document that represents an individual’s final wishes, this legal documents elaborates on how the estate of the deceased will be administered once they are dead or that who will manage their assets or property once they are no more. The types of Wills are:

  • Simple Wills
  • Testament Wills
  • Joint Wills
  • Living Wills

A Will is filed at The Master of The High Court by whosoever that is in possession of it, it doesn’t necessarily have to be the executor or the beneficiary. Drawing up a will ensures that the wishes of the deceased are known, that the administration of the estate takes place speedily and that disputes are avoided.
